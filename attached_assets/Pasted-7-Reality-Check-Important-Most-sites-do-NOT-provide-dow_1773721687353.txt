7. Reality Check (Important)

Most sites do NOT provide downloadable full trade histories.

Why?

Because they all do the same thing internally:

index blockchain
store trades in database
compute metrics
display summaries

But they rarely expose the raw dataset.

So your difficulty pulling data is normal.

Even experienced devs complain about Polymarket data access.

11. Important: How PolymarketAnalytics Actually Works

Sites like PolymarketAnalytics usually:

index every trade globally
store in postgres
compute wallet metrics

Meaning:

They don’t fetch trades per wallet.

They do:

fetch all trades
then filter by wallet

This is the key insight.
