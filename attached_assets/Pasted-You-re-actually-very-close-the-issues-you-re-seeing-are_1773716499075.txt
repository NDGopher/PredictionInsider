1. The Biggest Bug: You're Calculating PnL Wrong

You wrote that your system may be:

summing gross wins instead of net profit

That is almost certainly correct.

Your /closed-positions logic likely does something like:

total_wins = sum(win_amount)
total_losses = sum(loss_amount)

pnl = total_wins - total_losses

This is incorrect for Polymarket.

Polymarket payouts already include the stake.

Example:

Buy YES @ 0.40
Size = 100
Cost = $40

If win:
Payout = $100

Actual profit:

profit = payout - cost
profit = 100 - 40 = 60

If your code records:

win = 100
loss = 40

then:

100 - 40 = 60

which works.

But many APIs return:

payout = 100
loss = 0

Then your formula becomes:

100 - 0 = 100

2.5x inflated.

Multiply this across thousands of markets and you get numbers like:

kch123 = $57M

when the real number is:

$191K
Correct PnL Formula

You must compute:

profit = payout - total_cost

Where:

total_cost = sum(price * shares)

Final wallet PnL:

PnL =
    sum(SELL proceeds)
  + sum(REDEEM payouts)
  - sum(BUY cost)

NOT:

wins - losses
